Privacy Policy for Basketball Stats

Last updated: April 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Basketball Stats. As an independent developer, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how your data is handled when you use my application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Team names and information
  • Player statistics and performance data
  • Game records and match details
  • User preferences and settings
  • Team rosters and player lineups

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage statistics
  • App performance data
  • Error reports

2. How Your Information is Used

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To improve the app's performance and features
  • To provide user support
  • To fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is collected or stored on external servers
  • Your data remains private and is not shared with third parties

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Export your data from the app
  • Stop using the app and remove it from your device

5. Target Users and Professional Use

This app is specifically designed for basketball coaches and team staff members. While the app may store data about players of various ages, it is intended to be used exclusively by coaching staff and authorized team personnel. As a professional coaching tool, the app should be used in accordance with your organization's data handling policies and applicable sports regulations in your region.
